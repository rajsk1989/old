Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload an Excel file?

To upload an Excel file, go to the Dashboard page and click on the "Browse Files" button or drag and drop your file into the upload area. Supported formats include .xls, .xlsx, and .csv.

What Excel formats are supported?

Our application supports Excel files in .xls and .xlsx formats, as well as CSV files. For best results, we recommend using .xlsx format as it preserves all data formatting.

How do I download my report as PDF?

After processing your data, click the "Download Full Report (PDF)" button. The PDF will be saved to your device with a filename that includes the current date for easy reference.

Why is my revenue showing as negative?

A negative revenue value indicates that your total costs (marketplace fees, return charges, ads cost, etc.) exceed your total sales amount. Review your expenses to identify areas for optimization.
